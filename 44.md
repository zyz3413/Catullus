fui libenter in tua suburbana	
villa malamque pectore expuli tussim,	
non immerenti quam mihi meus venter,	
dum sumptuosas appeto, dedit, cenas.	
nam, Sestianus dum volo esse conviva,	
orationem in Antium petitorem	
plenam veneni et pestilentiae legi.

I have been gladly in your suburban house and I have pushed out from my chest a bad cough which my stomach has given to me not undeserving while I chase(d) after costly meals. For while I want(ed) to be a guest of Sestus, I have read (his) speech, full of poison and plague, against the candidate Antius.
